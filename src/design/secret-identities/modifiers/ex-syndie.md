## Concept

An **Ex-Syndie** gains a cache of random traitor gear, left behind from their past as an agent. They may use this gear in whatever way they see fit--generally in service of their regular secret identity objectives.

## Gameplay

Because traitor gear is often generically useful for accomplishing many goals, most secret identities can find a use for it. This creates ambiguity, as even a full set of some traitor secret identity's gear plus dubious actions isn't enough to conclusively pin someone as a traitor. It also creates an opportunity for traitors to pose as essentially any secret identity--if they get caught eventually, being seen drinking the entire bar and playing yourself off as an Ex-Syndie [Guzzler](../crew/guzzler.md) isn't the worst strategy.
