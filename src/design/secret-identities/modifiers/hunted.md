> **Description:** You are being hunted by someone else. Be wary.
>
> **Objectives:** Don't die to your hunter (or otherwise allow your hunter to do whatever they're trying to do to you).
>
> **Masquerade/secret identity notes:** Rollable randomly with a quite low chance, otherwise added dynamically by other identities or systems when needed
>
> *Huh? Who's there--oh, just a mouse. Ah!--uh, that was the announcement system. Yo Captain, what's go--GAWGHK*

## Concept

Very basic modifier. Added by certain things which have targeting kill-like objectives, like [Debt Collector](../crew/debt-collector.md), and provides deniability for which specific one it is (although its uh, just debt collector right now). Also can roll randomly and freak you out for no reason.

## Gameplay

Knowing that you are being hunted adds a large amount of paranoia to gameplay in a really fun way that allows you to strategize around that knowledge, and having it as an objective synergizes nicely with targets like [Secretary](../crew/secretary.md).

The random roll chance ensures that it's not 100% metagamable, and sometimes creates situations where you're paranoid about nothing, which is funny.
