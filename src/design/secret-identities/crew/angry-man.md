## Concept
The angry man is a secret identity that grows increasingly red and angry as other organizations complete their objectives.
Generally, as the other organization completes their early objectives, the man's anger is relatively low.
As the organization nears total completion, the man's anger grows more and more rapidly.
For something like traitors, their anger would only really become noticeable once the terminals were getting hacked.

## Abilities
The angry man's skin color represents enemy organization progress.
As the percentage of completed objectives grows, their skin turns more and more red.
When he reaches peak anger, he becomes fully enraged, speaking in all caps and gaining a buff due to his rage.

## Gameplay
The angry man serves as a humorous tattle for the progress of an enemy organization.
Since they can easily alarm others to when other organizations are advancing their goals, they become an extremely vocal alert as to the state of others.
Additionally, their burst of power at peak anger gives the crew a last-ditch effort to defeat any other organizations before they win.
