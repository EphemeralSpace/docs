## Concept

The VIP is a Very Important secret identity that starts with a VIP card with their name on it. This card does nothing other then acting as a meta-confirmation that they're the VIP, as only the VIP can spawn with the card. 

## Abilities

If the VIP is killed, an announcement will play lamenting their tragic loss, and several attack drones will spawn in vents around the station that will attack anyone who hurt the VIP on sight. They aren't *that* threatening, though.

## Gameplay
The VIP acts as a sort of "soft-safe-check" and sets the stage for social intrigue relating to its card. For example, the VIP might use their card to confirm their innocence and guarantee their safety at a crucial time... or maybe, someone's stolen the VIP's card and is trying to use it to do the same, which acts as a seed for further social intrigue.

The VIP's on-death effect is a soft punishment that is circumventable and able to be planned around, but still has a noticeable narrative effect on the round and thematically cements the VIP's important status.
