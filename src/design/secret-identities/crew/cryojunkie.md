## Concept

The Cryojunkie is a secret identity which revives on death as a weak [cryohusk](../../cryohusks.md), caused by an experimental anti-death implant that may or may not work as well as it's claimed.

## Abilities

Once per round, a Cryojunkie can transform into a cryohusk one minute after death. This will completely revive the Cryojunkie, but they will remain weak and unable to speak clearly at all for a short time until they get their bearings back.

## Gameplay

Though lacking concrete objectives, the Cryojunkie can have a massive narrative effect on how events play out in a round due to the game-altering ability to revive after death with almost no caveats. This ability being so powerful is the reason for not simply making it a modifier or trait: planning around the death revival is the Cryojunkie's main intrigue. Cryojunkies are one of the only ways to truly continue to have a direct effect on the round after death--even if being an obvious cryohusk makes things a little difficult. Frankly its simply freaky to watch your buddy get killed and then they come back as a fucked up blue ice zombie.

The Cryojunkie has some deniability with changelings (tba) and [corpsychids](../parasite/corpsychid.md), which both present in similar ways to cryohusks and have methods of reviving after death.

If a Cryojunkie is killed and then revives as a husk, objectives which require them to be killed will not fail (ic they're 'socially dead' in some way, I guess). This gives them novel interplay with other secret identities or organizations which may seek to kill a Cryojunkie, since one may volunteer their own life for a greater purpose or as part of a backdoor deal.
