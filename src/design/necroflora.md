# Necroflora

{{#template ../templates/unimplemented.md}}

Necroflora is a invasive plant colony which can rapidly infect a unsuspecting ship, station, or planet. It functions as a "Creep" style biome, slowly spreading and infecting the station, making areas it has infected more dangerous. As a radiotrophic organism, the radstorm is amplifying its growth. As the radstorm approaches closer, the Necroflora's spread, speed and potency increase.

# The Necroflora Threat

Necroflora is a map component suited for larger, 32 player maps. It aims to provide a more interesting obstacle to map design and an ever-present threat to rounds.
Map design wise, Necroflora is a way to make parts of the map more interesting and threatening to traverse, adding a cost to exploring deep maints locations and navigating to key utilities or high grade loot zones. It slows down the process of looting maints, making deep maints loot no longer "first come, first served".
Roundflow wise, Necroflora personifies the threat of the radstorm by itself becoming more dangerous as the radstorm approaches. Working to keep it contained adds another reactive crew task which requires more engaged coordination and intervention, while also rewarding crew for proactively organizing expeditions deep into Necroflora territory.
The design of the Floral Organs is intended to encourage working as a team to clear rooms out, and planning the method of clearing rooms carefully. Traversing into creep without a plan should feel like "bullet hell". You can dodge and weave, rewarding high skill, but one slip up can be very dangerous.
The Necroflora interacts with all three vectors of station decay in unique ways. Filth nourishes the Necroflora, speeding its growth up greatly when growing into a Filth filled area. Floral Creep generates water vapor when it meets fluids, creating the conditions for rust. Fire, on the other hand, is anathema to the highly flammable Necroflora, but wildly setting it on fire in the hopes of getting rid of it can have an equally detrimental effect to the station as having let it spread.


# Floral Creep

The foundation of Necroflora is the Floral Creep, a carpet which grows across floor tiles and can eventually overrun a whole station. Objects adjacent to or on top of Floral Creep may slowly degrade as the creep works its way through the station. Doors will be slowly forced open. Unattended lockers might become hosts to Floral Organs. Water tanks, jerrycans of fuel and blood spills are all greedily consumed and converted into water vapor while accelerating creep growth. Standing on Floral Creep isn't slowing or damaging, but its presence is a sign of danger.

Floral Creep and Floral Organs are immune to cold, suffocation and radiation, being adapted to survive in space. It can, however, be destroyed. Slashing it to pieces suffices, but fire, high temperatures and caustic chemicals can rapidly clear it from a room.

# Floral Organs

Certain suitable objects left unattended in a room with creep slowly become hosts for Floral Organs. Floral Organs typically serve as defense mechanisms for the creep, but some merely cause inconveniences for unprepared crew. Without exception, all Floral Organs are immobile.
- Lockers and Crates transform into Traplashes if there are none nearby, which shoot out vines as soon as they detect movement and pull whatever they hit inside them, locking shut. They can be tricked by putting another item in front of you, and will hold whatever they grab inside them for at least a minute. While in the case of getting caught you can break out from the inside, anything stuck inside will take damage over time.
- Vendors transform into Seedspewers, shooting out explosive seed pods whenever someone is standing on Floral Creep nearby.
- Gas Canisters transform into Pollensacs, constantly filling the room with mildly caustic pollen.
- Microwaves, Blenders, Faxes and other generic electronics transform into Thornnodes, which connect to nearby Thornnodes as well as other power drawing devices like APCs and Computers with thorny electrified vines.
- Corpses transform into Corpseblooms, shooting caustic pollen globs in a circular pattern at short intervals.
- Vents transform into Ventblossoms, which clog the vent, preventing air from being pumped in or out, and attack anyone moving nearby with damaging pollen glob projectiles.

Destroying a Floral Organ will leave the object it transformed from in a damaged but repairable state.

# Floral Events

While Floral Creep is on a station, the following events can appear.
- Floral Incursion: Floral Creep begins to expand through the ventilation system, growing adjacent to vents at a chosen location.
- Floral Haze: All Ventblossoms and Pollensacs immediately produce one time burst of pollen, and Ventblossoms start pumping Pollen into the atmospheric network for the rest of the round.
- Tearing Vines: Walls near to Floral Creep begin being damaged for a short duration.
- Thermophile Mutation: The Necroflora becomes immune to fire, consuming it to accelerate growth, but loses its cold immunity.
